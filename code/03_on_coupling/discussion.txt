Coupling

Vrem sa avem loose coupling, si low coupling.

Low coupling este cand depind de mai putine chestii.

Loose coupling este cand depind de ceva stabil (interfete).

In limbajele puternic tipizate (Java) loose coupling-ul se optine prin interfete si polimorifsm.
Limbajele dinamice (JavaScript) se bucura by default de loose coupling.

Design by contract (types) vs design by capability.
